Structural and Dynamical Crossovers in Dense Electrolytes

Daehyeok Kim, Taejin Kwon*, and Jeongmin Kim*

February 3, 2026 · Physical Review E

Using molecular dynamics simulations of primitive electrolytes with and without space-filling solvent particles, this work elucidates structural and dynamical crossovers that emerge with increasing salt concentration.

Explicit-solvent electrolytes exhibit a transition from a charge-dominated dilute regime to a density-dominated concentrated regime, accompanied by crossovers in ion self-diffusion and ion-pair lifetimes. The results identify local ion pairing and small aggregates as key microscopic origins of these changes.
